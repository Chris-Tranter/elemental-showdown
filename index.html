Elemental Showdown

Welcome young one!

In a six-choice game inspired by rock-paper-scissors and the Chinese philosophy of Wu Xing, the elements could interact as follows:

  1. Fire:

    • Defeats: Wood, Air
    • Defeated by: Water, Earth

  2. Air:

    • Defeats: Earth, Water
    • Defeated by: Fire, Iron

  3. Earth:

    • Defeats: Water, Fire
    • Defeated by: Wood, Air

  4. Water:

    • Defeats: Fire, Iron
    • Defeated by: Earth, Air

  5. Wood:

    • Defeats: Earth, Iron
    • Defeated by: Fire, Water

  6. Iron:

    • Defeats: Wood, Air
    • Defeated by: Fire, Earth
